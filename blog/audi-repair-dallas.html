Will Independent Service Void My Audi Warranty?

Under the Magnuson-Moss Warranty Act, Audi of America cannot void your warranty simply because you chose an independent shop for routine maintenance. The law requires Audi to demonstrate that independent service caused a specific failure before they can deny warranty coverage on that basis. We follow Audi factory service specifications — correct oil grades, approved fluid types, and proper service intervals — and document every visit so your records are complete.

How often should I service my Audi in Dallas?

Audi recommends oil service every 10,000 miles under normal conditions, with a more comprehensive Inspection service at 20,000-mile intervals. In Dallas heat and traffic, TFSI engines in particular benefit from fresh oil to minimize carbon deposits — we recommend not stretching these intervals.

Can you do a pre-purchase inspection on a used Audi?

Yes. On used Audis we specifically check for TFSI carbon buildup history, S tronic clutch pack wear, Quattro transfer case condition, and electronics issues. These are items that don't show on a test drive but affect long-term reliability significantly. We provide a full written report.
