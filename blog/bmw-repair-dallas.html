Will Independent Service Void My BMW Warranty?

Under the Magnuson-Moss Warranty Act, a vehicle manufacturer cannot void your warranty simply because you chose an independent repair shop for routine maintenance. As long as the shop uses parts that meet the manufacturer's specifications and follows the factory service schedule, your warranty coverage remains intact. If a dealer ever tells you otherwise, they are required by law to demonstrate that the independent service actually caused the defect they're refusing to cover — not simply that you didn't use their shop.
