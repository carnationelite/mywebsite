Will Independent Service Void My Ford Warranty?

Under the Magnuson-Moss Warranty Act, Ford Motor Company cannot void your warranty solely because you used an independent repair shop. The law requires Ford to demonstrate that any independent service directly caused the warranty-related failure — not just that you went somewhere other than a Ford dealership. We follow Ford's factory specifications for parts, fluids, and service procedures, and we maintain complete service records for every visit.

How often should I service my Ford in Dallas?

Ford's Intelligent Oil-Life Monitor tracks your actual driving conditions and alerts you when an oil change is due — typically between 7,500 and 10,000 miles for full synthetic. Dallas summer heat and heavy towing can shorten this interval, so we recommend following the monitor rather than a fixed calendar schedule.

Can you do a pre-purchase inspection on a used Ford?
