Will Independent Service Void My Honda Warranty?

Under the Magnuson-Moss Warranty Act, Honda cannot void your warranty because you used an independent shop for routine maintenance. The law is clear: a warranty denial requires the manufacturer to prove that the independent service caused the specific failure being disputed. Using an independent shop that follows Honda's specifications, uses appropriate parts and fluids, and maintains service records does not jeopardize your warranty coverage. We document every visit thoroughly.
