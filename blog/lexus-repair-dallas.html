Will Independent Service Void My Lexus Warranty?

Under the Magnuson-Moss Warranty Act, Lexus (Toyota Motor Corporation) cannot void your warranty because you used an independent shop for routine maintenance. The law requires Lexus to prove that independent service actually caused the failure before denying warranty coverage. We follow Lexus factory service specifications, use appropriate fluids and filters, and document every service thoroughly so your records are complete and verifiable.

How often should I service my Lexus in Dallas?

Lexus recommends oil service every 5,000 miles with conventional or synthetic-blend, or every 10,000 miles with full synthetic. Dallas summer heat and highway driving are factors, and we recommend following the Lexus Maintenance Schedule in your owner's manual rather than a generic interval.

Can you do a pre-purchase inspection on a used Lexus?

Yes. On used Lexus vehicles we pay close attention to hybrid battery health (on applicable models), air suspension condition, transmission fluid quality, and any deferred maintenance. Even a well-maintained Lexus benefits from a thorough third-party inspection before purchase. We provide a full written report.
