Will Independent Service Void My Mercedes Warranty?

Under the Magnuson-Moss Warranty Act, Mercedes-Benz cannot void your new vehicle warranty simply because you chose an independent shop for routine maintenance. The law requires that to deny a warranty claim, the manufacturer must demonstrate that the independent service actually caused the defect — not merely that you went somewhere other than a dealership. We follow Mercedes factory service specifications and document every service visit so you have complete records if a warranty question ever arises.
