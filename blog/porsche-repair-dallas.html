Will Independent Service Void My Porsche Warranty?

Under the Magnuson-Moss Warranty Act, Porsche AG cannot void your warranty because you chose an independent repair facility for routine maintenance. The manufacturer must prove that any warranty-related failure was caused by the independent service — not simply that you didn't use an authorized dealer. We adhere to Porsche factory service specifications, use appropriate fluids and parts, and keep thorough per-visit documentation so your service history is complete and verifiable.

How often should a Porsche be serviced?

Porsche recommends service intervals based on mileage and time — typically an Intermediate Service every 20,000 miles or 2 years and a Major Service at 40,000 miles or 4 years (whichever comes first). Dallas driving conditions and heat can affect fluid and filter life, so we recommend staying on schedule.

Can you do a pre-purchase inspection on a used Porsche?

Yes, and we strongly recommend it. Issues like IMS bearing wear on older 911s, coolant pipe failure on early Cayennes, or PDK wear are expensive and not obvious without a proper inspection. We provide a full written report covering all major systems.
