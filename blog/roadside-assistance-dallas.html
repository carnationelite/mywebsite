Whatever the situation, CarNation Elite provides true 24/7 roadside assistance across Dallas and the surrounding metro area. Signs You Need Roadside Assistance

  • Vehicle won't start — battery is dead, starter clicks, or there's no response at all when you turn the key
  • Flat tire and your spare is flat, missing, or a compact spare you can't safely drive on at Dallas highway speeds
  • Keys locked inside the vehicle — a genuinely common situation in a rush-hour-heavy city like Dallas
  • Engine temperature warning light or gauge climbing into the red, or steam rising from the hood
  • Vehicle stalled in a dangerous location — a live freeway lane, a busy intersection, or anywhere you can't safely wait for help

Frequently Asked Questions — Roadside Assistance Dallas

What exactly does your roadside assistance in Dallas cover?

We cover emergency towing, battery jump-starts and battery replacement, vehicle lockout service, flat tire changes, and emergency fuel delivery. If the vehicle can be repaired on the spot — a dead battery, a tire swap — we'll do it roadside. If it needs shop time, we'll tow it to our Floyd Circle location or wherever you direct us.

How quickly will you respond to a roadside call in Dallas?

Response times vary based on your location and current demand, but we aim to be on the way within 15–30 minutes for most Dallas and inner-suburb locations. When you call (214) 597-4922, we'll give you an honest ETA based on where you are — not an optimistic guess designed to keep you calm while you wait twice as long.

Do you cover all of Dallas and surrounding cities?

We serve Dallas and the surrounding metro including Garland, Mesquite, Richardson, Plano, and Irving. If you're further out in the DFW area, call us — we'll help directly if we can or point you to the fastest available resource for your location.

What should I do while waiting for roadside help on a Dallas freeway?

If at all possible, get your vehicle completely off the travel lanes — onto the shoulder, an exit ramp, or a surface street. Turn on your hazard lights immediately. If you're on a high-speed freeway like 635 or I-35E, stay inside your vehicle with your seatbelt buckled — it's significantly safer than standing outside near passing traffic. Keep your phone charged and in your hand. If you feel the situation is genuinely dangerous, call 911 first to request traffic safety assistance.

Can you tow my car to a different shop or the dealership if I prefer?

Yes. We'll tow your vehicle wherever you direct us. We're glad to bring it to our shop for diagnosis and repair, but we understand you may have a dealer warranty situation, a preferred mechanic elsewhere, or other reasons to choose a different destination. We'll tell you the pricing and options upfront so you can make the call.
