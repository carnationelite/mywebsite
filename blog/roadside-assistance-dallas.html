What to do when your car breaks down in Dallas

First, get to safety. If possible, pull completely off the roadway onto the shoulder or into a parking lot. Turn on your hazard lights immediately. If you're on a high-speed road and can't safely exit the vehicle, stay buckled inside with your hazards on and call for help from inside the car. Don't stand behind or beside the vehicle on the freeway.

Once you're safe, call CarNation Elite at (214) 597-4922. We'll get your location, assess what you need, and dispatch help as quickly as possible. We serve the entire Dallas metro — including Garland, Mesquite, Rowlett, Balch Springs, Sunnyvale, and surrounding communities.
