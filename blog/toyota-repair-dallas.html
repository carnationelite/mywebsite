Will Independent Service Void My Toyota Warranty?

Under the Magnuson-Moss Warranty Act, Toyota cannot void your warranty simply because you had maintenance performed at an independent shop. The law requires that Toyota demonstrate the independent service actually caused any failure they're declining to cover. Routine oil changes, filter replacements, and fluid services performed to Toyota specifications at a qualified independent shop do not affect your warranty. We document every service visit and use parts that meet Toyota's requirements.

How often should I service my Toyota in Dallas?

Toyota recommends oil changes every 5,000 miles with conventional oil or every 10,000 miles with Toyota's 0W-20 full synthetic. Dallas heat and stop-and-go traffic can affect this, and we recommend following Toyota's Maintenance Schedule in your owner's manual.

Can you inspect a used Toyota before I buy it?
