Coverage Timeline

Exactly What's Covered & When

We believe you deserve to know exactly what your warranty covers before you hand over your keys. No fine print designed to confuse. Here's our coverage in plain English.

1
Days 1 – 90
Full Parts & Labor Warranty
Any defect in parts we installed or work we performed is fixed or replaced at no charge — parts cost and labor both. This is our strongest coverage and it applies to every repair we do: brakes, engine, AC, transmission, and more.
2
Months 4 – 12
Extended Parts-Only Warranty
We continue to cover the parts themselves through month 12 from your service date. If a part fails due to a defect — not normal wear — we replace it. Labor charges may apply depending on the scope of repair, but you'll never pay for a defective part twice.
✓
Always
Transparent Communication
Before any warranty repair, we walk you through what happened, why, and what the fix is. We document everything. If we made a mistake, we own it. That's what it means to treat your vehicle like our own.
✅ What's Covered
  • Defects in parts installed by CarNation Elite technicians
  • Workmanship errors — if we did it wrong, we fix it free
  • Brake pad & rotor replacements, caliper work
  • Engine repair — gaskets, sensors, mechanical components
  • AC system repairs — compressor, refrigerant, leaks we repaired
  • Transmission service and fluid work
  • All general maintenance services performed at our shop
  • Electrical repairs and diagnostic follow-up
  • Any repair we recommended and you approved in writing
✗ What's Not Covered
  • Normal wear and tear unrelated to our repair
  • Damage from accidents, collisions, or external impact
  • Repairs performed by another shop after ours
  • Elective part replacements not linked to a diagnosed failure
  • Owner abuse, neglect, or improper fluid maintenance
  • Pre-existing conditions on unrelated vehicle systems
